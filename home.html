    SAVE DREAMS GROUP

    we should create a better world by joining hands together to bring universal change and smiles to the less fortunate in the society in africa.

    THE SAVE DREAMS GROUP-KENYA is a brain child of Fidelis Musyoka. Having observed a lot of what com-rades go through in the university, I thought it was wise to come up with a way of helping the students in a comrade for comrades’ initiative. I believe We can’t control our cradle but we can dream and engineer our dreams to change our Destinies. In the recent past, we have had students dying from suicide and others suspending their studies due to financial difficulties for food and upkeep or fees. For me to allow this to continue will be a great failure. When life has been lost due to the above mentioned and school suspended herein we should see it as an opportunity to save a dream that has been lost. I WANT TO ACT NOW.

inspiring a generation.

To sustain dreams by offering support in any form.
